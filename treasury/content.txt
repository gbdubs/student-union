		<h2>P-Card Tips and Rules</h2>
		<br>
		<p>
			If it is related to purchasing food at a restaurant, you MUST have:
			<ol>
				<li>A Food Waiver</li>
				<li>An itemized invoice including all the products you will be purchasing, delivery fee, tax, and tip</li>
				<li> Proof in SUMS that you have the funding for the specific event you are purchasing food for</li>
			</ol>
		</p>
		<br>
		<p>
			If it is for purchasing items that you will be using for your club online you MUST have:
			<ol>
				<li>The links you will be using</li>
				<li>Proof in SUMS that you have the funding for the specific events/activities you are purchasing items for</li>
			</ol>
		</p>
		<br>
		<p>
			If it is for purchasing items that you will be using for your club not online (via phone call) you MUST have:
			<ol>
				<li>An itemized invoice including all the products you will be purchasing, delivery fee, tax, and tip</li>
				<li>Proof in SUMS that you have the funding for the specific event you are purchasing for</li>
			</ol>
		</p>
  
		<br>
		<p>
			If it is for paying for fees (fees for people you hired, space you rented, etc.) that you will be using for your club online you MUST have:
			<ol>
				<li>The links you will be using</li>
				<li>Proof in SUMS that you have the funding for the specific event you are purchasing for</li>
			</ol>
		</p>
		<br>
		<br>
		<br>
		<h2>Check Policies</h2>
		<br>
		<h5 class="light">Getting checks from the treasurer  - Checks from Bank of America are for EMERGENCIES ONLY</h5>
		<br>
		<ol>
			<li>Things you can use the checks for -- Last minute problems that have arisen regarding club events that can only be solved through immediate cash.</li>
			<li>You cannot use checks to buy food from the grocery store</li>
			<li>You cannot use checks to pay for any fees to attend certain events for your club (tournaments, learning opportunities, etc.) (These fees should go through a PRF, not a check.  If you come to the treasurer for a check for these reasons, you will be told that you are not able to do this.  Please plan accordingly, and submit your PRFs for this, 2-3 weeks in advance of the event you need the money for.)</li>
		</ol>
