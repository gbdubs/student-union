Charles River (Grad) Senator

Valarie Timms
vtimms@brandeis.edu
Valarie Timms is a senior from Baltimore, Maryland. She is a double major in politics and American studies with a double minor in journalism and legal studies. Outside of student union she is an admissions ambassador and enjoys doing theater. Valarie got involved in the student union because she felt a strong need to give back to the Brandeis community that has given so much to her. As the Charles River/ 567 senator, her goal is to represent the needs and opinions of her fellow residents and work together to affect real change on the issues they find most important.
