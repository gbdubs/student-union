September 3rd Elections Information


Your Student Union

We are here to serve you from orientation to graduation and beyond. How?

Advocacy

We are your link to change. No matter your concern or idea, we have connections within the alumni, administration, faculty and trustees to address your concerns and ideas. Learn more about the Executive-Board and our partnerships with all of the members of the Brandeis Family.

Allocations

The Student Union fills our campus with life through the financial support of our vibrant clubs and associations. The Allocations board makes all funding decisions for our student run organizations, trying to maximize the impact of your organizations. Learn more about A-Board here.

Community

We take pride in our community, and in its stewardship. The Student Union works to promote a safe, respectful, and intentional student environment, creates and reviews student focused laws and initiatives, and builds community within and around our campus. Learn more about the Senate here.
